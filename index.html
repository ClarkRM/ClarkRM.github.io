Problem Statement: Pet Food

Our users are having difficulty finding the right food for their pet. With so many options, it is difficult to find food that fits both the pet's nutritional needs and the owner's price point.

Affinity Diagram: Pet Food

We brainstormed everything we could think of regarding pet food, diet, and app usability. There are features for owner convience, for pet diet, and for other users to communicate their own opinions.
